We are aware of the dangers of using pesticides, and it’s harm that it can do to the environment. However, We can assure you that our methods of pest extermination won’t harm the environment in any manor. The pesticides we use can be found naturally in the environment like Diatomaceous Earth, Boric acid, Pyrethrin, Mentha x piperita etc. These Pesticides can be used to exterminate pests and deter rodents from entering your home, all without endangering the environment at all.
